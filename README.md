## Objectives

The objective of this project is to create an AI model capable of predicting employees that are likely to leave the company by choice and understand the factors causing the departure. The main challenge is to make sure that the model respect AI Explainability and AI Ethics principals.

## Scope

The project is led over the course of two days in a group of five engineering students. Four out five of the participants are Majoring in data and artiffical intelligence and one in Connected Objects and Cybersecurity.
Our goal is to have a model that is both evaluated highly in terms of performance metrics such as accuracy and f1-score as well as in terms of bias indicators and other ai explainability tools. 
We seek to follow the guidelines imposed by the AI ACT as well as respecting the [AIF 360 (Artiffical Intelligence Fairness 360)](https://github.com/Trusted-AI/AIF360) norms. 

The deliverables expected at the end of the two days are:
  - Clear Visualisations explaining data bias
  - Working model Pipelines evaluating both performance and ethics
  - The following github composed of:
      - The project src files
      - The current ReadMe
      - Technical Documentation on the project
      - Architecture overview
      - A video demonstration
      - Presentation Slide
   - A clear Presentation of our product

The question of frugality and security fall out of the scope of this specific project however if the project should be taken up again these are two elements that can and should be improved upon.

## Project Persona

Our project is constructed for the HR department of a company that is struggling with a large number or resignations and / or that is struggling to understand the motivation behind employee resignations.
